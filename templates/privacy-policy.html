Privacy Policy

SAT India is committed to protecting user privacy and maintaining transparency in how information is handled. This platform follows a privacy-first and consent-driven approach.

Privacy-First Principle

SAT India does not collect or process personal information unless it is strictly necessary, lawful, and purpose-limited.

Information We Do Not Collect

SAT India does not:

  • Monitor individuals or devices
  • Track users in real time
  • Collect personal data without consent
  • Access private communications or content
  • Perform surveillance activities

Minimal Data Usage

Where data is required for lawful or operational purposes, only the minimum necessary information is processed, and only for the intended purpose.

Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use invasive tracking technologies or third-party advertising trackers. Basic technical cookies may be used solely to ensure proper website functionality.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organisational safeguards are implemented to protect any information handled by the platform from unauthorised access, misuse, or disclosure.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Any updates will be published on this page.
